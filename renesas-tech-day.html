Renesas Tech Day at SHIFT

Renesas and Rabyte teams visited the Lumax SHIFT facility to showcase their automotive chip portfolio and a range of live demos across next-generation mobility technologies.

Event 11 June 2026 · SHIFT Facility
Event Jun 11, 2026 · SHIFT Team
Overview

On 11 June 2026, the Renesas and Rabyte teams visited the Lumax SHIFT office and showcased their automotive chip portfolio along with a range of live demos related to automotive technologies. Renesas Tech Day 2026 was held at the SHIFT facility, bringing together teams for technical discussions, live demonstrations, and collaboration on advanced automotive technologies. The event provided valuable insights into semiconductor solutions, haptics, telematics, and vehicle electronics, while fostering strong technical interactions and innovation in next-generation mobility solutions.

Highlights
  • Showcase of the Renesas automotive chip portfolio for next-generation vehicle platforms
  • Live demonstrations spanning semiconductors, haptics, telematics, and vehicle electronics
  • Technical discussions and deep-dive sessions between the Renesas, Rabyte, and SHIFT teams
  • Collaboration on advanced automotive technologies and next-generation mobility solutions
  • Strong technical interactions fostering innovation across the SHIFT engineering tracks
Participating Teams
  • Renesas — automotive semiconductor solutions and live technology demos
  • Rabyte — distribution partner supporting the automotive chip showcase
  • Lumax SHIFT — host facility and engineering teams across delivery tracks
