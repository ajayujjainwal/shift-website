R&D Feb 10, 2026 · Satish Keni
LiDAR vs Camera Fusion for ADAS Perception — Key Trade-Offs for SDV Use Cases
A technical comparison of LiDAR-only, camera-only, and fusion-based perception pipelines for Level 2+ ADAS. We evaluate detection range, object classification accuracy, BOM cost, and robustness in adverse weather. Our finding: for Indian road conditions at L2+ ADAS, a camera-first fusion strategy with radar supplementation offers the best cost-performance trade-off. LiDAR adds marginal benefit below 80 km/h urban scenarios.

R&D Jan 15, 2026 · Satish Keni
Edge Inference Optimization: Getting DMS Under 150 ms Latency
Achieving real-time driver monitoring on a cost-constrained embedded platform required significant model compression. This note documents the path from a 480 ms baseline latency to under 150 ms using INT8 quantization, layer fusion, and hardware-specific NPU tuning. We also discuss the accuracy trade-offs at each compression step and how we recovered accuracy lost in quantization using QAT (Quantization-Aware Training).
