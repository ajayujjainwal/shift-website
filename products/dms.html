Overview

The SHIFT DMS (Driver Monitoring System) is an in-cabin AI system that continuously monitors the driver's eye gaze, head pose, and facial expressions to detect distraction, drowsiness, and emotional state. Designed for Tier 1 OEM integration, it runs on a compact embedded compute module and outputs AUTOSAR-compatible alerts over CAN/Ethernet.
