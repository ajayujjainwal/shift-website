Overview

The SHIFT Motor Controller is a compact, high-efficiency inverter and control unit for electric vehicle drivetrains. It implements field-oriented control (FOC) algorithms for BLDC and PMSM motors, delivering precise torque response across the full speed range. Designed for both low-voltage (48V) mild-hybrid and high-voltage (400V) full-EV platforms, it integrates natively with CAN FD vehicle networks and supports ISO 15118 charge communication.
