Overview

The SHIFT USB-C Multimedia Connectivity Hub is the first product in the portfolio to be formally kicked off. Developed in partnership with A2C Automotive as a JV between IAC and SHIFT, it provides a multifunction USB-C interface for front console integration ‚Äî simultaneously handling smartphone data connectivity (Android Auto, Apple CarPlay), fast device charging, and vehicle diagnostics over the same port. Designed to replace legacy USB-A connectors across all trim levels for Mahindra and MSIL.

Key Features
  • USB-C 3.2 Gen 1 data ‚Äî native Android Auto & Apple CarPlay connectivity
  • USB Power Delivery (PD) ‚Äî up to 45W fast charging without a separate charger port
  • OBD / diagnostics passthrough ‚Äî service and diagnostic access over USB-C
  • Multifunction: data + charge on a single port, replacing dual USB-A + charge sockets

  • Automotive-grade connector rated for 10,000+ insertion cycles
  • Localized connector footprint ‚Äî compatible with Mahindra & MSIL console tooling
