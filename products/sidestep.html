Overview

The SHIFT Side Step Module is a smart, electrically actuated running board engineered for safety, durability, and ease of use in SUVs and premium vehicles. Developed in partnership with Synadyne as a JV between SHIFT and Lumax Accessories, the module deploys automatically on door open and retracts when closed, improving ingress/egress accessibility for all passengers ‚Äî including elderly and children. All safety interlocks (speed, obstacle, anti-pinch) are integrated into the embedded controller, and the unit is validated for harsh road and climate conditions.

Key Features
  • Automatic deploy / retract triggered by door-open signal from body ECU
  • Speed interlock ‚Äî step locks retracted above threshold speed for safety
  • Obstacle detection ‚Äî proximity sensor prevents deploy onto obstructions
  • Anti-pinch protection ‚Äî reverse actuation on load obstruction detection
  • 750 kg load rating ‚Äî supports all passenger profiles
  • IP67-rated actuator and wiring harness for all-weather durability
  • CAN / LIN bus integrated ‚Äî step status visible in instrument cluster
