SBW Gear Shifter

Software-defined Shift-by-Wire shifter with functional safety, diagnostics, and OEM HMI differentiation — enabling the transition from mechanical shifters for EVs and premium ICE vehicles.

Body Electronics Concept Ready
Overview

The SHIFT SBW (Shift-by-Wire) Gear Shifter is a software-defined electronic shifter that replaces the mechanical gear selector with a purely electronic system — enabling gear changes via switch, rotary dial, or touchpad HMI inputs with no physical cable or mechanical linkage to the gearbox. Developed in partnership with A2C Automotive as a JV with LMAT (Lumax Mannoh Allied Technologies), it targets EVs and premium ICE platforms for M&M and Tata. With OEM-configurable HMI and AUTOSAR-based diagnostics, the platform enables strong brand differentiation through software features.

Key Features
  • Full electronic Shift-by-Wire — no mechanical cable or linkage required
  • OEM-configurable HMI: toggle switch, rotary, touchpad, or haptic pad
  • ISO 26262 ASIL-B/C functional safety — redundant position sensing and actuator paths
  • Integrated diagnostics and FOTA — remote configuration and SW updates
  • AUTOSAR-compatible CAN FD interface for body domain ECU integration
  • Electric park mode — automatic P selection on key-off or door open
  • Anti-roll interlock — prevents D/R gear selection unless brake is applied
