SBW Gear Shifter

Software-defined Shift-by-Wire shifter with functional safety, diagnostics, and OEM HMI differentiation ‚Äî enabling the transition from mechanical shifters for EVs and premium ICE vehicles.

Body Electronics Awaiting Budget
Overview

The SHIFT SBW (Shift-by-Wire) Gear Shifter is a software-defined electronic shifter that replaces the mechanical gear selector with a purely electronic system ‚Äî enabling gear changes via switch, rotary dial, or touchpad HMI inputs with no physical cable or mechanical linkage to the gearbox. Developed in partnership with A2C Automotive as a JV with LMAT (Lumax Mannoh Allied Technologies), it targets EVs and premium ICE platforms for M&M and Tata. With OEM-configurable HMI and AUTOSAR-based diagnostics, the platform enables strong brand differentiation through software features.

Key Features
  • Full electronic Shift-by-Wire ‚Äî no mechanical cable or linkage required
  • OEM-configurable HMI: toggle switch, rotary, touchpad, or haptic pad
  • ISO 26262 ASIL-B/C functional safety ‚Äî redundant position sensing and actuator paths
  • Integrated diagnostics and FOTA ‚Äî remote configuration and SW updates
  • AUTOSAR-compatible CAN FD interface for body domain ECU integration
  • Electric park mode ‚Äî automatic P selection on key-off or door open
  • Anti-roll interlock ‚Äî prevents D/R gear selection unless brake is applied
