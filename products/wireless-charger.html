Wireless Charger Qi 2.1

Automotive-grade fast wireless charging with Qi 2.1 Magnetic Power Profile ‚Äî inhouse development for smartphone charging across all trims, targeting Mahindra & Hero.

Charging In Planning
Overview

The SHIFT Wireless Charger is an inhouse-developed automotive-grade wireless charging module built to the Qi 2.1 Magnetic Power Profile (MPP) standard. It delivers significantly higher charging speeds compared to legacy Qi 1.3 pads while maintaining compatibility with existing Qi-enabled devices. Designed for center console integration across all trim levels for Mahindra and Hero, the module addresses the market shift toward faster wireless charging as a standard feature in Indian vehicles.

Key Features
  • Qi 2.1 Magnetic Power Profile (MPP) ‚Äî up to 15W fast wireless charging
  • Backward compatible with Qi 1.2 / 1.3 devices at 5W/7.5W
  • Foreign Object Detection (FOD) ‚Äî prevents charging metal objects, reduces waste heat
  • Thermal management ‚Äî automotive-grade heatsink, operates to +85¬∞C ambient
  • Slim profile ‚Äî fits flush in center console and armrest tray designs
  • Charging status LED strip with configurable brightness via LIN bus
