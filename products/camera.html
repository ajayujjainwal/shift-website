Camera Platform

Scalable camera hardware platform covering Surround View System (SVS) and Driver Monitoring System (DMS) sensing ‚Äî ADAS, parking, and driver monitoring for MSIL & Tata.

ADAS & Safety In Planning
Overview

The SHIFT Camera Platform is a scalable, automotive-grade camera hardware foundation that underpins both Surround View System (SVS) and Driver Monitoring (DMS) sensing. Developed in partnership with CANLab and Edgeverse, the platform offers common hardware interfaces, ISP pipeline, and calibration tooling ‚Äî enabling ADAS, parking assistance, and driver monitoring use cases to share a validated camera stack. Targeted at MSIL and Tata programmes requiring Level 2 ADAS functionality and parking compliance.

Key Features
  • Common scalable camera HW for SVS (surround view) and DMS (driver monitoring) use cases
  • Multi-camera array support ‚Äî up to 6 cameras on a single ECU fabric
  • Wide dynamic range imaging ‚Äî HDR sensor for day/night and tunnel conditions
  • NIR + RGB dual-mode support for DMS sensing in low-light environments
  • Shared ISP pipeline with per-camera calibration and lens distortion correction
  • FPD-Link III / GMSL serialization for camera-to-ECU long-reach cabling
  • AEC-Q100 Grade 2 components, IP67/IP69K housing options
